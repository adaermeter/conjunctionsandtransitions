What are Transition Words?

Transition words connect sentences and paragraphs, making your writing more fluent and organized. They show relationships between ideas.

ADDITION

Used when adding information to a sentence:

Also
Furthermore
Moreover
In addition
Besides
What's more

Examples:

• She speaks Turkish; in addition, she knows English.

• He is intelligent. Furthermore, he works very hard.

CAUSE & EFFECT

Therefore
Consequently
As a result
Thus
Hence
Accordingly

Examples:

• It was raining heavily. Therefore, the match was canceled.

• He didn't study. As a result, he failed the exam.

COMPARISON & CONTRAST

However
On the other hand
Conversely
In contrast

Nevertheless

Indeed

Clearly

TIME
