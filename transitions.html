What are Transition Words?

Transition words connect sentences and paragraphs, making your writing more fluent and organized. They show relationships between ideas.

Academic Writing Transitions

Adding evidence: "The data supports this conclusion. Furthermore, recent studies confirm these findings."

Showing contrast: "The theory appears sound. However, practical applications reveal limitations."

Drawing conclusions: "The results were consistent across all tests. Therefore, we can accept the hypothesis."

Business Communication

Formal reports: "Sales increased by 15%. Consequently, we can invest in new equipment."

Email communication: "The meeting is postponed. Meanwhile, please review the attached documents."

Presentations: "We've covered the financial overview. Now, let's discuss marketing strategies."
